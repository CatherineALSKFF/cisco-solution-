# Commercial Exposure Analysis Standards

Identify financial and operational risks in vendor contracts.

## 1. Pricing & Cost Risks

**Red Flags:**
- No price cap or benchmark clause
- Unlimited price increases allowed
- No most-favored-customer (MFC) pricing
- Hidden fees or uncapped overage charges
- No volume discount tiers

**Required Terms:**
- Annual price increase cap (e.g., max 3-5% or CPI)
- Price benchmarking rights
- Transparent fee structure
- Volume-based discounts

## 2. Minimum Commitments

**Red Flags:**
- High minimum annual commitment (MACs)
- Use-it-or-lose-it terms
- No rollover of unused credits
- Penalties for underutilization
- Auto-escalating minimums

**Required Terms:**
- Reasonable minimum commitments
- Credit rollover provisions
- Right to reduce commitment on renewal
- Clear utilization metrics

## 3. Payment Terms

**Red Flags:**
- Payment due before service delivery
- No right to withhold disputed amounts
- Excessive late payment penalties (>1.5%/month)
- No invoice dispute process
- Net-15 or shorter payment terms

**Required Terms:**
- Net-30 or Net-45 payment terms
- Right to dispute invoices
- Reasonable late payment terms
- Clear invoicing requirements

## 4. Liability & Indemnification

**Red Flags:**
- Unlimited Cisco liability
- One-sided indemnification (Cisco indemnifies vendor)
- No liability cap for vendor
- Consequential damages exposure
- IP indemnification gaps

**Required Terms:**
- Mutual liability caps
- Vendor IP indemnification
- Exclusion of consequential damages
- Carve-outs for willful misconduct, IP, confidentiality

## 5. Termination Costs

**Red Flags:**
- High early termination fees
- No termination for convenience
- Locked-in multi-year terms
- Exit fees or transition costs uncapped
- Data hostage clauses

**Required Terms:**
- Termination for convenience with reasonable notice
- Pro-rated refunds on early termination
- Capped transition assistance fees
- Data portability rights

## 6. Auto-Renewal Traps

**Red Flags:**
- Short notice period for non-renewal (<90 days)
- Auto-renewal at increased rates
- Multi-year auto-renewal periods
- Silent renewal (no notice required)

**Required Terms:**
- 90-180 day non-renewal notice window
- Renewal at same or lower rates
- Annual renewal terms (not multi-year)
- Written renewal confirmation required

## 7. Audit Rights

**Red Flags:**
- Unlimited vendor audit rights
- Short audit notice period (<30 days)
- Cisco pays audit costs regardless of findings
- Audit frequency >1x per year
- Broad audit scope beyond license compliance

**Required Terms:**
- Cisco audit rights over vendor
- 30-45 day advance notice for audits
- Audit costs borne by party at fault
- Maximum 1 audit per 12-month period
- Defined audit scope

## 8. Change of Control

**Red Flags:**
- Vendor can assign without consent
- No protections on vendor acquisition
- License terms change on acquisition
- No notice of ownership changes

**Required Terms:**
- Assignment requires Cisco consent
- Termination right on vendor change of control
- Terms survive acquisition
- Notice of material ownership changes

---

## Risk Scoring

| Exposure Level | Criteria |
|----------------|----------|
| **High (Red)** | Unlimited liability, no price caps, unfavorable audit, termination traps |
| **Medium (Yellow)** | Some protections but gaps exist |
| **Low (Green)** | Standard commercial protections in place |
