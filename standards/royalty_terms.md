# Royalty Terms & Conditions Review Standards

Analyze royalty structures for risk and compliance.

## 1. Royalty Structure Clarity

**Red Flags:**
- Ambiguous royalty calculation method
- Undefined "net revenue" or "gross revenue"
- Unclear unit of measurement
- Multiple overlapping royalty triggers
- Subjective determination clauses

**Required Terms:**
- Clear royalty base definition
- Specific calculation methodology
- Defined unit of measurement (device, user, seat, etc.)
- Single, unambiguous trigger event

## 2. Royalty Rate Fairness

**Red Flags:**
- Rates significantly above market benchmarks
- No rate reduction for volume
- Rates increase over time without justification
- Compound royalty stacking
- Royalties on internal use/testing

**Required Terms:**
- Market-competitive rates
- Volume discount tiers
- Rate stability or decrease over time
- No royalty stacking
- Exemptions for internal development/testing

## 3. Reporting Obligations

**Red Flags:**
- Excessive reporting frequency (weekly/daily)
- Undefined report format
- Unreasonable reporting deadlines
- Penalty for minor reporting errors
- Retroactive reporting requirements

**Required Terms:**
- Quarterly or monthly reporting (not more frequent)
- Defined report template
- 30-45 day reporting window
- Good faith error correction period
- Prospective reporting only

## 4. Audit Rights (Royalty-Specific)

**Red Flags:**
- Audit with <30 days notice
- Auditor not independent (vendor employee)
- Audit covers unlimited historical period
- Cisco pays all audit costs
- Punitive underpayment penalties (>10%)

**Required Terms:**
- 30-45 day audit notice
- Independent third-party auditor
- Audit period limited (2-3 years)
- Loser-pays for significant discrepancies
- Reasonable underpayment interest (prime + 2%)

## 5. Payment Terms

**Red Flags:**
- Payment due before reporting period ends
- No dispute mechanism
- Wire transfer only (no ACH/check)
- Currency conversion at vendor's discretion
- Late fees >1.5%/month

**Required Terms:**
- Payment due 30-45 days after report
- Invoice dispute process
- Multiple payment methods
- Fixed currency conversion methodology
- Reasonable late fees (prime + 2%)

## 6. True-Up & Reconciliation

**Red Flags:**
- Annual true-up with interest on underpayment only
- No over-payment credit/refund
- True-up deadline too short
- Vendor-determined true-up calculation
- No reconciliation statement

**Required Terms:**
- Mutual true-up (over and under payments)
- Credits or refunds for overpayment
- 60-90 day true-up period
- Joint reconciliation process
- Written reconciliation statement

## 7. Royalty Caps & Minimums

**Red Flags:**
- High minimum royalty regardless of usage
- No royalty cap or ceiling
- Minimum increases automatically
- Use-it-or-lose-it minimums
- No floor on per-unit rates

**Required Terms:**
- Reasonable minimum commitment
- Annual or lifetime royalty cap
- Minimum tied to actual usage
- Credit rollover for unused minimums
- Rate floors (minimum per-unit protection)

## 8. Termination & Wind-Down

**Red Flags:**
- Royalties due after termination
- No sell-off period for inventory
- Perpetual royalty obligations
- Retroactive royalty claims
- No final reconciliation process

**Required Terms:**
- Royalties end on termination date
- 90-180 day sell-off period
- Clear termination of royalty obligations
- Final reconciliation within 60 days
- Release of future claims

---

## Royalty Risk Scoring

| Risk Level | Criteria |
|------------|----------|
| **High (Red)** | Unclear calculation, excessive rates, punitive audits, no caps |
| **Medium (Yellow)** | Some terms unclear or unfavorable |
| **Low (Green)** | Clear, market-rate, balanced terms |
